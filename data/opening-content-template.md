# Opening Content Template

Use this template when adding a new opening to `trainingCatalog`.

## Intro

- `whyThisOpening`: 2-3 sentences about practical fit and player profile.
- `history`: 2-4 sentences with historical context and modern viability.
- `viability`: 1-2 sentences tying opening to quiz profile signals.

## Variations (minimum 3, ideally 4)

Each variation should include:

- `id`: stable key (e.g. `foundation`, `main`, `tournament`, `master`)
- `label`: clear player-facing title
- `summary`: short description of identity
- `style`: positional | balanced | tactical
- `risk`: low | medium | high
- `theoryLoad`: light | medium | heavy
- `tempo`: practical pace description
- `middlegamePlans`: 2-4 concrete plan bullets
- `fitSignals`: 2+ audience-fit bullets
- `sampleLine`: one short representative line description
- `timeControlFit`: which time controls this branch suits
- `commonMistakes`: at least 2 common errors to avoid

## QA checklist

- Intro fields are complete and non-empty.
- Variation cards are complete and balanced.
- At least one line lesson is tagged for each branch.
- Lesson prompts and answers match board/line data.
